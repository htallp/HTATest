Partnership

Our ability to bring people together makes our projects special. From the early stages of involvement, we develop a unified vision, delivering true Localism and shared value for everyone involved. We understand the needs and cultures of different stakeholders, from local communities to Local Authorities, Government bodies and commercial business. Projects

We invest in substantial, game-changing development alongside communities at the local level. Innovative design and sustainability are promoted. We establish legacy companies to manage our developments, engage with our partners and bring benefits to the local community. All of this unlocks value and planning potential to create beautiful places for people to live, work and play.

We invest in substantial, game-changing development

Royal Hospital Haslar

We are sponsoring the redevelopment of the historic former Royal Hospital Haslar site in Gosport, Hampshire, which we acquired from the Ministry of Defence in 2009. We led the bid to success and assembled operators, a construction and development partner and substantial investment to deliver a ground-breaking new scheme for Haslar that includes a world-class retirement community for veterans.

Mixed use, mixed generations and mixed communities are our hallmark.

www.royalhaslar.com

Wilton Hill

Our Enterprise has formed a partnership with Redrow Homes Ltd and the Wilton Community Land Trust to create a new neighbourhood at the former Erskine Barracks site in Wilton, near Salisbury, Wiltshire.

This 36-acre development is tailored to Wilton. It involves genuine community engagement and has excellent sustainability credentials. The creation of a new Enterprise Hub places employment and training at the heart of the site, led by an innovative partnership involving the community. A ‘veterans village’ on the site, with supported housing, employment and training facilities, meets an important local need in an area with a strong connection to the Armed Forces.

www.wiltonhillpartnership.co.uk
